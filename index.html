Typography & voice

Headlines: Playfair Display / Crimson Pro / Georgia-family serif.

Body: Lato / Source Sans 3 / system sans (Segoe UI, Roboto etc.).

Brand voice: Warm, grounded, and practical. The message should feel like guidance from someone who understands life transitions — not sales language or hype.

Dev notes

Implementation tokens

Suggested design tokens consistently used across Cindy projects:

--color-sage: #2F6F63; --color-forest: #2E5E4E; --color-earth: #8B5E3C; --color-gold: #D8A34A; --color-coral: #C7644F; --color-cream: #F4EFE7; --radius-lg: 22px; --radius: 14px; --shadow-soft: 0 10px 26px rgba(0,0,0,.08);

Keep buttons rounded, use soft shadows (no harsh drop shadows), and prefer warm off-white backgrounds over pure white for large sections.

Awakening Path — deeper voice

For Cindy This is your more direct, awakening-focused voice for people already questioning old patterns.

It speaks to people who feel that life, or the world around them, no longer adds up — and who are ready to move from confusion to clarity, and from clarity to action.

Key ideas from the page

  • Direct philosophy: Many people have lived inside beliefs they never truly chose.
  • The uncomfortable middle: Real transformation often means sitting with discomfort instead of escaping it.
  • What this path is for: people stuck in loops, drowning in distraction, or feeling disconnected from their own truth.
  • Goal: not to keep people comfortable inside patterns, but to help them learn to see and move differently.
Usage & lanes

Where this voice belongs

Soft entry lane (public, broad):

  • Homepage headline and packages.
  • Flyers and simple ads.
  • Intro emails and booking pages.

Awakening Path lane (deeper):

  • Long-form pages and blog posts.
  • Talks, podcasts, and retreat materials.
  • Follow-up content for people already engaged.
